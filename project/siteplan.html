Website name: Nature's way

Site Name

Name:naturesway.org

Organization's name:Ikpemhi"s Green Movement

This name represents an educational platform that promotes the health benefits of natural fruits and vegetables. It conveys the core message of healthy living through nature's bounty.

Another option:greenmovement.org

Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to educate visitors about the health benefits, uses and components of natural fruits and vegetables. The site will detail the nutritional components of various fruits and vegetables, their uses, benefits, and include links to reliable sources on where and how to get them; It will also show users how to use them for our benefits. It aims to help increase the use of nature fruits and vegetables, thus reducing some health issues and sickness.

Scenarios

  • Which fruits and vegetables are best for boosting the immune system?
  • What fruits helps with digestion?
  • Can I grow Onions in my garden>
